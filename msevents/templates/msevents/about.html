The New Gates

CMU and its Big Footprint

Pittsburgh is changing: it's now known as the Seattle of the East. Large tech companies are invading Pittsburgh and CMU's backyard. Google, Disney, Boeing, and Facebook are all in the area and all competing for the top talent at CMU.

Microsoft's Relationship with CMU

CMU yields the most hires per any University in the world for Microsoft. In 2016 alone we have 58 summer interns from CMU, 70 percent of which are Full-time eligible. Our technology (Kinect) is used in all entry level CS classes. However, with big competitors coming to Pittsburgh, we have to get creative with our imprint on the campus.
