Spaces and Technology

This space was created with the idea that “if we don’t have good internet, we don’t have anything.” The space makes use of a variety of different technology for student, visitor, and faculty use. The space has 3D printers, a video wall, Fios Internet, dual monitor docking stations and a Sonos sound system.

Main Room

Lounge-type room with couches and relaxed seating. This can be rearranged to comfortably fit 50 people. Great for tech talks, lounging, meetings, content display, xbox tournaments, demos. Includes:

  • 2 couches
  • 5 bag chairs
  • 9 screen video wall w/ sonos sound system
  • Track lighting on two opposite corners for highlights on speaker/demo
  • Curtained off meeting room that seats 8, with chalk wall

Lab

63ft-long room with standing work space and nook with relaxed seating and 72inch tv w/ sonos capability. Great for working, building, lounging, large group hacks, team or club meetings Includes:

  • 40ft of stainless steel standing work space
  • three 3d printers
  • seating along stainless steel work bench, around table, and nook

Office

Three separate closed work spaces attached to lab. Great for working privately and quietly. Includes ( per office ):

  • Two sit/stand desks
  • 2 30” HD monitors per desk
  • 1 docking station per desk
  • Whiteboard
